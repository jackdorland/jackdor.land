who am i?

  • Hey! I'm Jack, also known as Kai, and I'm a political science student turned pen-and-pencil nerd. You can often find me at numerous local coffee shops in the New Jersey / New York City area, with my favorite right now being Turnstile Coffee Roasters in Belmar, New Jersey. Show them some love if you're in the area!
  • In my free time, I work on a longform journalistic research project called "With Whom We Confuse Ourselves," an inside look into the youth's mental health crisis via interviews, poems, expert opinions, and analysis. I'm also a programmer specializing in Haskell and Rust development - and a lifelong Linux user.
  • I also enjoy playing a laundry list of video games. Presently, I'm regularly playing Escape from Tarkov, Splatoon 3, Rainbow Six: Siege and Apex Legends.

    • Rotring 800 in 0.5mm with a tape mod
    • Multiple Pentel Energel Infree pens in 0.5
    • Pentel Kerry P1037 in 0.7mm
    • Pilot FriXion Ball Knock Zone Wood Grip in 0.5mm
    • Pilot Kuru Toga mechanical pencil in 0.3
